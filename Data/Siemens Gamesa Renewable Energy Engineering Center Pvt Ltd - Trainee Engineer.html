Description :

We at Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy, a leader in Renewable Energy, provide solutions to customers all over the globe. As a key player and innovative pioneer in the sector of renewable energy, we have installed our products and technology in more than 90 countries, with a total capacity base of over 84 GW.  

With over 25,000 employees we work to create diverse product portfolio from 2.1 MW in Onshore turbines to 8  MW in Offshore turbines.  

With a responsibility of being a leader in renewables and making a key contribution towards decarbonization,  when people come to work here they know they have a mammoth challenge ahead of them, “To make  renewable energy on par with traditional energy sources” 

In a diverse mix of portfolio, it takes the brightest of minds to innovate constantly and make a difference to  people’s lives. At Siemens Gamesa it takes imagination to create green energy for the generations to come  and make real what matters 

Towards big goal of achieving a sustainable and competing renewable energy, we are in lookout for Brightest  Mind like you from Engineering Institutions of repute, who can work on our Next Gen Programs and be “Young  Leaders for Change” 

We are looking for Post graduate engineers from Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical, and  Aerospace Engineering streams, from reputed universities, who possess a very good level of analytical and quantitative aptitude.
