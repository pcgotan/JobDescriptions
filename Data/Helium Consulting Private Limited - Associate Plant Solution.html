Dear Team, 
 
We are writing to you from Helium Consulting- a young IIT-ian founded company with a rich bank of expertise in the Chemical and Hydrocarbon process industry. Housed in one of the premiere process and chemical engineering centres of India at National Chemical Laboratories (NCL) Innovation Park, under the aegis of CSIR- India's premier research body, Helium has consistently, worked on important and challenging problems in all our areas and industries of focus.   We work with cutting edge software tools to create operational excellence in the Process Industry including Data and Optimization tools including advanced control, analytics and supply chain software’s.   We are hiring outstanding fresh Chemical engineering grads with 4-yr B.Tech with interest in working in the domain while having a keen interest in software. You should have excellent analytical Skills, sound knowledge of the basics of Chemical engineering and basic software exposure including programming and use of automation tools like excel. We will match your knowledge with some of the most challenging problems we are trying to solve for our customers through implementation of software that will bring Operational Excellence and other related benefits to their manufacturing. We work with some of the top names in process industry - be it refining, gas, chemicals, specialties, pharma or polymers- several of our customers being Fortune 1000 companies. You will work on some of the most advanced and modern software platforms. You will go through the journey of applying and working on latest software and applications.    About your job role: Associate, Engineering Solutions - •    Understand the Industry landscape in our markets of choice. •    Learn new software in the technologies we work in. •    Contribute to the team through your own knowledge and innovative thinking.     You will travel and be mentored by an outstanding group of individuals and team members with wide and varying experience in the industry. We work in a great environment- one of the greenest campuses in Pune in the heart of academic district surrounded by NCL, IITM, IISER etc and offer some of best facilities in terms of work environment, excellent facilities and benefits like medical insurance for you and your family. 
 
Our HR team will be in touch with you, in case you have any query can reach us on hr@heliumconsulting.com or can directly call on +91 20 6520 9880
