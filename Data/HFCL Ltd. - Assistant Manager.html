Company Name : HFCL Ltd.
Nature Of Business : Telecom Infrastructure & Optical Fiber Manufacuring
Designation : Assistant Manager
Tentative Job Location : Delhi NCR
Description :

Engineers hired from campus shall undergo 5 months rotational training program and 7 months on the job training as a part of PGET Spark Program.

 

The objective of the one year training program is to groom young engineers into technical and business leaders by giving them an overview of the organization, actively involving in projects, sites, plants and NOC visits. On successful completion of 1 year training, PGETs shall be inducted as Assistant Managers.

       Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Work in the action learning projects assigned by the Project Directors, Team Leaders and as the part of the rotational program
  • Frequent interaction and presentation to the Leadership team
  • Active involvement in live projects related to telecom infrastructure, Railways, Defence, smart city, manufacturing units
  • To provide strategic inputs to smooth line the entire manufacturing process
  • Active involvement in day to day business meetings
  • Submission of project reports to the assigned mentors and supervisors

 

Eligibility Criteria:

M.Tech in ECE/EE/Mech

Key Desired Skills:

  • Enthusiastic, vibrant, energetic, youthful, hardworking
  • Adaptable and flexible to travel
  • Openness to learn
  • Good analytical and logical skills
  • Positive attitude/Forward looking attitude
  • Good presentation and communication skills
