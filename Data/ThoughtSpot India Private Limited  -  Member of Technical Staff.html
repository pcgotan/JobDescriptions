Job Description

 

Come join our team of world-class innovators and builders, who are on a mission to create a more fact-driven world. Our platform, based on our pioneering Search & AI technology, is one of the fastest adopted analytics products in the global enterprise market. As a result, we are a leader in the BI Gartner magic quadrant for two years running, and the fastest company to enter the leader quadrant. We work hard at making deep technology available to users with a simple, yet smart experience - be it one of the fastest in-memory analytical databases in the world that we’ve built from scratch or our AI engine which asks questions on behalf of our users. That’s why customers like Walmart, BT, Siemens, Daimler, Exxon, Hulu, Royal Bank of Canada, Bank of the West, and Nationwide Building Society have turned to ThoughtSpot to transform their decision-making cultures. 

We are looking for engineers with a proven track record of being industry visionaries to join the team responsible for building ThoughtSpot’s natural language relational search engine, personalized data exploration engine, universal object search engine, AI-powered ranking, and relevance engine distributed in-memory search indexing infrastructure and much more.
