Overview and Role Brief

 

Selected Project Trainees (PT’s) will undergo 9-12 months training and induction program in various functions of the company –Operations and Quality, Information Technology, Data Analytics, Sales, Market Research, Finance & HR. During each stint, PT’s will gain an excellent understanding of how each department functions and at the end of all rotations, they will have a good overview of the business and organization functioning.

 

During each functional rotation, the PT’s will work on live projects of organizational importance. After the end of each rotation, the PT’s will make presentations basis the live projects / learnings to the top management.

 

At the end of all rotations, the organization will make the best effort to place the PT’s basis their interest area and manager feedback. However, the organization decision will be final on matter of such final functional placements.

 

During the functional rotations, the PT’s responsibilities also include the coordination with hands on approach and completion of projects on time, summarize project progress, prepare reports. They will coordinate with other departments to ensure all aspects of each project are working harmoniously.

 

Requirements:

 

  • Zeal / Passion to make the difference with new innovative ideas and approach.
  • Strong Analytical Ability
  • Attention to detail and multi-tasking skills
  • Learning and executing mindset in a VUCA world
  • Strong co-ordination and influencing skills
  • Ability to keep cool in a fast paced always ON environment
  • Strong working knowledge of MS Office
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
