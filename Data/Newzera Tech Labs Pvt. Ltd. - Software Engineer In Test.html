About the Job Role

There are all kinds of love. But if your love for coding beats all others and you find an unpressing joy in reviewing a system's code, then you’re the one awaited. So if your armory includes skills of identifying issues while processing codes at a high speed, foresight for a bigger scale and no bugs or faults can escape your expert eyes, then apply right away!

Minimum qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience
  • Experience programming in one of the following: Python, Java and/or C++
  • Experience with black box or white box testing
  • Experience debugging software and/or hardware
  • Experience with testing/releasing networking products.
  • Knowledge of test SW development fundamentals and code management.
  • Experience with test methodologies, writing test plans, creating test cases and debugging.
  • Hands-on experience with test planning, test designing and execution, performance and stress testing, implementing and maintaining quality test automation of both client and server systems..
  • Demonstrated experience to multitask and manage competing priorities
  • Demonstrated experience to balance independence tasks/goals while contributing to team goals and collaborating with other team members
  • Demonstrated creativity and initiative to improve testing coverage and effectiveness
  • Knowledge of industry standard test automation tools & automation frameworks.

Preferred Qualifications

  • 1-3 years of  experience in testing and debugging Web or Mobile Application for iOS or Android
  • Demonstrated experience with Black Box test planning and execution for both Functional Testing as well as E2E Release Testing
  • Experience in building test automation solutions, automated internal frameworks, monitor systems, or improving testing effectiveness.
  • Experience with the following coding languages: JavaScript or Shell.
  • Strong development management or testing management experience with a proven track record in scaling highly technical teams.
  • Experience with tools in Android Test Support Library used for UI test automation.
  • Knowledge of system components, interfaces, and protocols on mobile devices such as touch, display, camera, audio, cellular, wi-fi, bluetooth, network protocols, power and battery, system performance, multimedia, sensors, NFC, USB, Type-C,SPI, I2C, I2S, UART, etc.
  • Ability to lead testing activities and engagement with cross-functional teams to shape design and project direction.
  • Experience working with remote teams

Responsibilities

  • Create and implement high-quality automated tests to verify release readiness
  • Design and implement automated test frameworks and tools to fill test gaps
  • Investigate automated test failures to ensure high signal from our automated tests
  • Track effectiveness and coverage of automated tests
  • Partner with infrastructure and feature engineering teams to ensure automation is fully enabled
  • Independently drive goals as a project manager - tracking and reporting on status, escalating risks, and finding solutions to obstacles
  • Contribute or lead engineering efforts from planning, execution and delivery to solve complex engineering problems in tools and testing.
  • Develop scalable and reliable automated tests for software and feature quality at both system and user level.
  • Design, create, modify and execute test strategies/plans and drive to improve software testing processes and methodologies.
  • Optimize and maintain team oriented tools or tests to increase productivity.
  • Take responsibility for monitoring product development and usage at all levels with an eye toward improving product quality.
