Title : Embedded Engineer 

Location: Hyderabad

Experience level : 0-3 years

 

This is an open invitation for India’s best in class AI  Product Engineering to join the disruptive technology and thought leader in the Facial Recognition. If you live & breath engineering by solving not only engineering problems but are passionate about building state of the art products that help a billion users to evolutionize the human experience with locks and doors by joining the world class product team, then welcome to the Techolution!

 

Skills Required: 

 

  1.  Excellent problem solving & dealing with ambiguous/undefined problems; ability to think abstractly
  2. Prove experience in microcontrollers, preferably STM / Nordic.
  3. Expertise in RTOS and strong foundation in Embedded C programming. Zephyr experience is preferable. 
  4. Experience in working with PIC, ATMEL, AVR, ARM based SOCs.
  5. Protocols Like UART, SPI, I2C, CAN, MODBUS, TCP/IP, USB, Bluetooth.
  6. Experience in modules like Wi-Fi, BLE, Lora WAN, ZIGBEE, RF etc.
  7. Coordinate cross-functionally to insure project meets business objectives and compliance standards
  8. Proficiency in PWM, controlling motors and driver level programming.
  9. Strong experience in EEPROM, FLASH memory etc.,
  10. Experience in board bring-up, hardware level debugging. 
  11. Strong organizational skills and the ability to prioritize and work with great efficiency.
  12. Excellent written and oral communication and presentation skills and the ability to express thoughts logically and succinctly.
  13. Demonstrated ability to achieve stretch goals in a highly innovative and fast paced environment.
