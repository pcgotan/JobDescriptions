About Edgeverve

We steer the Digital Transformation with our AI-Enabled Solutions. Automation, Artificial Intelligence and Applications - these are the three tenets of our A Cube strategy. We believe that together these enable seamless digital transformation for businesses.

At EdgeVerve, we are making constant strides towards transforming enterprises by providing AI enabled Business Applications, leveraging the Infosys Nia™ Platform with capabilities across the Automation continuum. With the advent of cognitive automation, we believe this three pronged strategy will drive our clients to the future. This strategy will help our clients navigate their digital journey with our AI enabled Automation and Business Solutions.
